Microphone Required

To recite and test your memory using **Listen Mode**, please grant microphone access to this website.

How to allow on Android (Chrome):
  1. Tap the Lock/Settings icon 🔒 on the left side of the address bar.
  2. Select Site settings or Permissions.
  3. Toggle Microphone to Allow.
  4. Refresh the page and try again.
How to allow on iPhone (Safari):
  1. Tap the "aA" or Settings icon in the browser bar.
  2. Go to Website Settings.
  3. Set Microphone access to Allow.
GUIDE

💡 How HifzNoor Works

Master the art of Quran memorization using our modern interactive tools

1

📖 Listen & Read Along (Auditory Layer)

Click on any Ayah on the Mushaf page to highlight it and play professional audio recitations. Use the loop controls at the bottom to repeat single verses, a page, or active thematic groups to absorb the Tajweed.

2

🙈 Hide Text (Active Recall)

Click Hide Text or Hide Selection in the sidebar. This overlays a clean parchment cover on top of the text, forcing you to recite from memory. If you get stuck, look to the right margin—animated blue arrow hints (←) point directly to the hidden verse's starting word to guide you.

3

🎙️ Live Speech Recognition (Self-Correction)

Click the microphone button and recite. The speech recognition engine matches your voice offline. Correctly recited words automatically reveal themselves in clean black ink right over the calligraphy. Any mispronounced or omitted words remain hidden.

4

✍️ Tracing & Handwriting (Motor Memory)

Toggle Trace & Write Mode to draw directly on top of the page. Adjust opacity to trace the Uthmani letters, cementing the shapes, spelling, and spelling of the verses into your permanent visual memory.
